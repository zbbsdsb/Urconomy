3. **Mental Concept Overwrite**: A cognitive phenomenon in which an individual, due to insufficient literature review or lack of awareness of intellectual history, mistakenly attributes a concept—already articulated in the past but previously unknown (or forgotten) to them—as the product of their own original thinking. This process is often accompanied by a strong sense of "epiphany," followed later by a "déjà vu shock" upon encountering the prior work: *"Someone already said this—long ago!"*
