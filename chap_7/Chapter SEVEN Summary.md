### 7. Dreammakers

The metaverse lies quietly in its coffin—and sooner or later, we’ll split it open with the hardest axe we can find. Sitting atop Mount Teide in Tenerife, watching the sunset, we refuse to believe everything we see might simply be a Markov process predetermined at the moment of the Big Bang. But that doesn’t matter. Free will, the nature of the virtual—topics we once dared not touch—may now, through the metaverse, bring us face-to-face with the essence of reality far sooner than we imagined.
